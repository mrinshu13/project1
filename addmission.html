Admission Procedure

The registration for admission in basic classes begins on the Ist March , On the reporting day both the parents are requested to accompany their ward for the final talk. After selection the payments has to be paid w hin the stipulated period. On failing which the admission stands conceited.Admission will be made according to the vacancies existing in different courses & the child has to clear the entrance exam to seek admission in any of the course.Admission forms are attached to the prospectus available at our office of a payment of Rs. 100 /- only. Duly filled forms are accepted with the true copies of following documents.

Upto 1st Standard

Birth Certificate issued by municipal corporation or other such Civil Body.

The child should have at least five and half (5 /2) years of age for l" std.

Onward 1st Standard

Birth Certificate issued by municipal corporation or other such Civil Body.

The child should have at least five and half (5 /2) years of age for l" std.
